CASSAVA LEAVES

DESCRIPTION

This is a traditional food in the Liberian culture, the leaves grow in Liberia and its mostly prepard by the Gola tribe of Liberia, the tradinational name is called "kpasajama".

INGREDIENT

  • Dry fish
  • meat
  • chicken
  • ground cassave leavf
  • pepper
  • salt
  • oinion
  • seasons
  • cube
  • oil

INSTRUCTION FOR PREPARATION

  1. make the steam genrously and seasoned the meat
  2. Cooked the meat
  3. steam the cassava leaves
  4. Add all the ingredient including the meat and chicken and add water, but exclude the palm oil
    (Note: allow it cook for atleast 20mins
  5. Add the palm oil two minutes before taking off
  6. cooked it over medium heat until all water is absorbed (stired frequently)
  7. Remove from the heat, and its ready to serve
