POTATO GREENS

DESCRIPTION

This food is popular among the Liberia tribe, they love it because they grow it and its easy to prepard, it is also made up of the traditional pal oil,
the meal is usually made in two form, with red palm oil or with argo oil

INGREDIENT

  • Dry fish
  • meat
  • chicken
  • Potato greens
  • pepper
  • salt
  • oinion
  • seasons
  • oil

INSTRUCTION FOR PREPARATION

  1. watch the potagreens thorughly, remove the steam and slise it into small pecies
  2. seasoned the meat and chicken with onion cooked it in a seprate pot
  3. with the chicken if neccessary with an agro oil
  4. After the chicken fried, put the potagreens in the oil until it become tender,
    (Note: stir it frequently and add a pinch of baking sodawhile frying for color
  5. combined all of the ingredient into the pot, season it and add enough water to just
  6. cooked it over medium heat until all water is absorbed (stired frequently)
  7. Remove from the heat, and its ready to serve
