Palava Sauce

DESCRIPTION

This food is popular among the Liberia tribe, they love it because they grow it and its easy to prepard, it is also made up of the traditional pal oil,
the meal is usually made in two form, with red palm oil or with argo oil

INGREDIENT

  • Dry fish
  • meat
  • chicken
  • Potato greens
  • pepper
  • salt
  • oinion
  • seasons
  • oil
  • maggi

INSTRUCTION FOR PREPARATION

  1. Cooked the meat and chicken include season, onion and maggi
  2. Clean the palava source and remove from the steam
  3. Boiled the palava sauce with salt
  4. heat the oil over a medium heat
  5. Add the meat/ chicken into the palava sauce pot and season well
  6. Add the palm oil
  7. Remove from the heat, and its ready to serve
